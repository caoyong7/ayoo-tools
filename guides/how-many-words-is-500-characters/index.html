Writing guide

How many words is 500 characters?

In English, 500 characters is usually about 70 to 100 words. The exact number depends on average word length, punctuation, and whether spaces are counted.

A practical estimate

A short product description, profile bio, or search snippet with 500 characters often lands near one compact paragraph. If the text uses many short words, it can hold more words. If it uses longer technical words, it will hold fewer.

When character limits matter

Character limits are common in forms, SEO fields, ads, bios, and social posts. If a platform counts spaces, every blank between words is part of the total. If it excludes spaces, the visible letters and punctuation matter most.
